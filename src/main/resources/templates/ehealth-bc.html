eHealth — BC PharmaNet

PharmaNet Conformance Standards v3.7 — 19 Messages (12 Mandatory, 5 Optional, 2 PCR)

Mandatory Transactions 12
Required for PharmaNet conformance — all implemented in enrichment service
Adjudicate Dispense Claim (TAC/TDU)
HL7 v2
Submit a dispense claim (TAC/TDU 01/04) to PharmaNet for adjudication with drug utilization evaluation.
Claim Reversal (TAC/TDU 11)
HL7 v2
Reverse a previously submitted dispense claim. Must reference the original transaction.
Patient Keyword Maintenance (TCP)
HL7 v2
Maintain or update the patient keyword associated with the PharmaNet patient record.
Adjudication Reconciliation (TDT)
HL7 v2
Reconciliation of adjudication transactions (codes 30/31/32/33). Retrieves totals for balancing.
Get Location Details (TIL)
HL7 v2
Retrieve details for a PharmaNet-registered location (pharmacy, clinic).
Prescriber Identification (TIP)
HL7 v2
Identify a prescriber by practitioner ID or name. Returns address, phone, and registration details.
Patient Profile Info Update (TPI)
HL7 v2
Update patient profile: adverse reactions, clinical conditions, discontinue dispense, or add comments.
Patient Profile Request (TRP)
HL7 v2
Retrieve the patient's full medication profile from PharmaNet (last 14 months).
Retrieve Prescription (TRX X0)
HL7 v2
Retrieve prescription details by prescription ID, prescriber, status, or date.
Record Prescription (TRX X1)
HL7 v2
Record a new prescription on PharmaNet with full drug, dosage, and prescriber details.
Update Prescription Status (TRX X2)
HL7 v2
Update the status of an existing prescription (cancel, suspend).
Adjust/Adapt Prescription (TRX X3)
HL7 v2
Record a pharmacist adaptation with rationale, patient consent, and follow-up plan.
Optional Transactions 5
Specialized workflows — 5 of 12 optional transactions implemented
Medication Update (TMU 01)
HL7 v2
Update a patient's medication record in PharmaNet.
Medication Update Reversal (TMU 11)
HL7 v2
Reverse a previously submitted medication update.
Patient Address Update (TPA)
HL7 v2
Update the patient's address information in PharmaNet.
PHN Assignment / Lookup (TPH)
HL7 v2
Supports assignment or lookup of a PHN for patient identification workflows.
Patient Name Search (TPN)
HL7 v2
Search for a patient by name when direct identifying details are incomplete or unavailable.
HL7 v3 — Provincial Client Registry (PCR) 2
BC HCIM Client Registry — patient demographic lookups via HL7 v3
Find Candidates (HCIM)
HL7 v3
Search the Provincial Client Registry for patient candidates by name, DOB, gender, or PHN.
Get Demographics (HCIM)
HL7 v3
Retrieve complete demographic information for a patient from the PCR by PHN.
